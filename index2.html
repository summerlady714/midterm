Do's and Don'ts As A Rideshare Driver

  1. Keep a clean vehicle.
  2. Have amenities!
  3. Be open to giving the passenger the aux.
  4. Have EZ pass.
  5. Make sure the temperature is comfortable for you and your passengers.
  6. Don't talk politics!
  7. Learn to use the GPS without sound.
  8. Be aware of strange invites after the ride.
  9. Know where bathrooms are in your typical areas.
  10. Keep a cool head.

Do's and Don'ts As A Rider

  • Put on your seatbelt.
  • Always ask if it's ok to sit up front.
  • Be polite!
  • Don't talk politics!
  • Call to see how far your driver is willing to go.
  • Don't ask to fill the car beyond capacity
  • If you get the aux, make sure your music is cool.
  • If you choose the pool option, expect other riders.
  • Don't hit on the driver.
  • Tip your driver.
